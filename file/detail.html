Hey there. Guys,

we’ve got exciting news for you. Lately, we have been working on redesigning an app for tracking the sleep of a baby.

Come and check it out what we’ve done down below. Lullaai simplifies the way you understand and manage your baby’s sleep so the whole family can have healthy sleep habits. Lullaai is based on building healthy sleep habits combining the best research, baby sleep coach expertise, relaxation techniques, and the power of technology to approach baby’s sleep in every way. The Perfect Sleep mode calms your baby with lullabies, nature sounds and white noise. Also, if your baby wakes up, it will automatically reply with white noise to help them sleep and to calm them.
